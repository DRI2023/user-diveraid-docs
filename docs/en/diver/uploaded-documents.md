# Diver: uploaded documents

## Where to find it

Menu: **Diver -> Uploaded documents**

## Uploaded documents

This is where you find documents associated with your profile (typically uploaded/registered under your account).

![Uploaded documents](../../assets/screenshots/diver/en/documents-uploaded.png)

Typical steps:

1. Open the list.
2. Filter or search (if available).
3. Open a document to review details.

## Upload a document

Typical steps:

1. Click **Add a document**.
2. In **Document type**, choose the type (see below).
3. Upload the file (PDF/JPG/PNG).
4. Fill in the required fields (depending on the type).
5. Save, then check the document in the list.

## Which document type should I choose?

- **General document:** any general file (misc/supporting document).
- **License (crossover):** certifications useful for a crossover.
- **Diver medical certificate:** medical certificate for a diver.
- **Professional medical certificate (Divemaster/Instructor):** medical certificate for professional divers.
- **Professional insurance:** professional insurance proof.

## Common issues

- Document not visible: it may not be associated to your profile yet, or you may not have access.
- Download does not start: reload the page or check browser download/security settings.
