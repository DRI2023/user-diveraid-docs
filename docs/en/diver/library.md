# Diver: library

## Screenshot
![Library](../../assets/screenshots/diver/en/library.png)

## Purpose

The **Library** collects your learning materials (for example course books), depending on what is available for your profile.

## Where to find it

Menu: **Diver -> Library**

## Typical steps

1. Open **Library**.
2. Select an item (course/book).
3. Open it or download it (depending on the available actions).

## Common issues

- No content visible: you may have no associated items yet.
- Redirected to login: session expired.
